With more than 40 rides, slides, and attractions, Yas Waterworld promises a day filled with aquatic adventures and thrills. The park is inspired by the Emirati heritage and culture, featuring attractions like the world's first hydro-magnetic tornado waterslide and the largest surfable sheet wave in the Middle East.

Spread across an area of over 15 hectares, Yas Waterworld boasts a variety of attractions ranging from adrenaline-pumping slides to relaxing lazy rivers. Visitors can explore themed zones like Marah Fortress, Al Raha River, and Dawwama, each offering unique experiences and adventures.

Yas Waterworld is renowned for its innovative attractions and commitment to safety and guest satisfaction. Whether you're seeking high-speed thrills or family-friendly fun, Yas Waterworld has something for everyone. Located on Yas Island, Abu Dhabi, Yas Waterworld is a must-visit destination for waterpark enthusiasts and families looking for an unforgettable day of fun in the sun.
